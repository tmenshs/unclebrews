Our Story · Est. 2012

All We Have Is a Good Cup and Good Company

This is how Uncle Brew began — from one small stall and a family syrup recipe, to Quix Brew, the way our neighborhood orders milk tea today.

Uncle Rey · the first stall, 2012
Humble

It started with a stall, a shaker, and one stubborn recipe

Uncle Brew didn't open with a grand plan. It opened with Uncle Rey, a shaker he practically wore out, and a brown sugar syrup recipe he refused to shortcut — no matter how many times someone told him powdered mix would be faster and cheaper.

He believed a good milk tea only needed a few things done properly: real tea leaves, syrup cooked low and slow, and pearls with actual bite. Everything else was just noise. That belief is still the whole business plan.

Hand-shaken, every single cup
Honest

We don't just say fresh — we actually do fresh

A lot of milk tea brands like to say "fresh" and "homemade" on a sign. At Uncle Brew, it's just how the kitchen runs: syrup made in-house, tea brewed daily instead of stockpiled, and pearls cooked in small batches so nothing sits around losing its bite.

No shortcuts, even when shortcuts would've been cheaper and easier. If a cup has our name on it, it has to earn that name first.

The neighborhood that grew with us
Homegrown

We grew because our neighbors told their friends

There was no big marketing budget in the early years — just regulars who kept coming back, and kept bringing someone new with them. That's the only reason a single stall turned into a full menu, a real store, and eventually its own delivery app.

So when we built Quix Brew — our own ordering system with GCash payment and live rider tracking — it wasn't to reinvent the brand. It was to make the same cup easier to reach, for the same people who got us here.

Our promise

Taste the Uncle Brew difference

Whether you're catching up with friends, treating yourself after a long day, or just craving something sweet at 3pm — we want every cup to feel like the same one Uncle Rey handed out from that first stall. Crafted with love. Brewed with care. Every single time.
